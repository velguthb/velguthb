Understanding the Star-Formation Histories of Tidal Streams and Stellar Halos in the M81 Group. Working with Dr. Eric F. Bell.

  • Determined the star-formation cutoff time for both the tidal stream and core of F8D1, the nearest Ultra-Diffuse Galaxy to Earth, to uncover details about its interaction history with other M81 group members.
